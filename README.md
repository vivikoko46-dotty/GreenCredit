# GreenCredit - Carbon Credit Trading Platform

A decentralized carbon credit trading and environmental impact tracking platform built on Stacks blockchain. GreenCredit enables transparent, verifiable carbon credit transactions and environmental impact measurement.

## Features

- **Carbon Credit Trading**: Peer-to-peer carbon credit marketplace
- **Impact Verification**: Verifiable environmental impact tracking
- **Credit Issuance**: Certified carbon credit creation and issuance
- **Offset Tracking**: Track carbon offset activities and results
- **Marketplace Functions**: Buy, sell, and trade carbon credits
- **Verification System**: Third-party verification of environmental claims
- **Reporting Dashboard**: Comprehensive environmental impact reporting

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions

- `issue-carbon-credits`: Issue new verified carbon credits
- `trade-credits`: Execute carbon credit trades
- `retire-credits`: Retire credits for offset purposes
- `verify-impact`: Verify environmental impact claims
- `create-offset-project`: Create new carbon offset project
- `update-project-status`: Update project verification status

### Read-Only Functions

- `get-credit-details`: Retrieve carbon credit information
- `get-user-portfolio`: Get user's credit portfolio
- `get-market-price`: Check current market prices
- `get-project-details`: Get offset project information
